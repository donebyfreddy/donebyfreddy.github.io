How did I learn how to get good at Python?

During my Higher Level Cycle in Systems Administration and Network, I learned the basics of Python, which sparked my passion for development. 

I started small, working on personal projects, which steadily prepared me for bigger challenges.

My first major project, Stockify, was a stock control management system built entirely in Python using CustomTkinter for GUIs and CX_Oracle for database integration. Though I struggled at first, persistence made the process easier over time. 

Later, I had the opportunity to work with a research organization on data scraping and automating ticket purchases for the Real Madrid website.

In short, start small, stay persistent, and aim high!
