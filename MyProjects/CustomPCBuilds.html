Custom PC Builds

Before pursuing my Microcomputer Systems and Networks diploma, I sought to fund my driver's license by building and selling computers. 

Inspired by @sdfxshow, a YouTuber who specialized in PC building and repair, I began by purchasing a desktop from eBay, diagnosing and fixing an initial issue on my own. 

This hands-on experience deepened my understanding of computer components and systems. I then progressed to building three custom PCs from scratch, sourcing each part individually, and successfully selling them at a profit.

Conclusions

Embarking on the journey of custom PC building presented initial challenges, particularly when I encountered a desktop with an unknown issue. 

However, by immersing myself in this unfamiliar territory and persistently working to identify and resolve the problem, I gained a deeper understanding of PC construction and components.

This experience not only marked my entry into the IT world but also allowed me to merge my technical skills with entrepreneurial aspirations. Creating functional, high-quality systems while generating income has been rewarding. 

Ultimately, this endeavor reinforced my belief that embracing challenges leads to significant personal and professional growth, and I look forward to refining my skills and exploring new opportunities in technology.
