Why am I creating this project?

As a certified AWS Solutions Architect (Associate) and Microsoft Azure Administrator, I have developed a solid theoretical understanding of cloud technologies through my certifications in AWS and Azure. 

However, I believe that hands-on experience is essential for truly mastering these platforms. 

The Azure Resume Challenge is an opportunity for me to apply my knowledge in a practical context, refining my skills to better prepare for real-world scenarios in cloud environments. 

This project will enhance my expertise and ensure I am fully equipped to excel in future cloud-based roles.
